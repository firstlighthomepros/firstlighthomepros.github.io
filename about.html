Our story

Creating warm homes, one room at a time

FirstLight Home Pros was founded on a simple idea: your home should feel as good as it looks. We believe that fresh paint and expert craftsmanship transform not just walls, but the way families and couples experience their spaces. Serving the Tampa Bay area, we work with professional painting crews who are committed to quality work, honest pricing, and the kind of service that builds lasting relationships.

Every FirstLight Home Pros project is handled by skilled painting crews who take pride in their craft. We carefully vet and coordinate the painters we work with so you get consistent quality, reliable scheduling, and a clean job site every time — without the guesswork.
