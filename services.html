Answers about our painting services

Find answers to common questions about FirstLight Home Pros painting services. Reach out if you need more help.

Yes. FirstLight handles complete interior painting projects for living rooms, bedrooms, kitchens, and bathrooms, as well as exterior work including siding, trim, doors, and fences. We match each project to the right techniques and materials for lasting results.
Timeline depends on project size and scope. A single room typically takes 2 to 3 days. Whole-house projects or exterior work may take 1 to 2 weeks. We'll provide a clear estimate and schedule before we start.
Our process includes surface preparation, priming when needed, multiple paint coats for coverage and durability, and thorough cleanup. We protect your furniture and floors, and we work to minimize disruption to your home and family.
Absolutely. We're happy to discuss color options, finishes, and what works best for each room based on lighting and wear. We can also provide samples so you see how colors look in your space before we commit to a full project.
Yes. We use professional-grade paints and materials built to last in Florida's climate. Quality products mean better coverage, durability, and a finish you'll be happy with for years. We're transparent about what we use on your home.
Pricing depends on the size of the area, surface condition, paint type, and labor. We provide free estimates so you know the cost upfront with no surprises. Contact FirstLight Home Pros to schedule your consultation and get an accurate quote.
